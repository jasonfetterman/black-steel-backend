Service Deployment Agreement (SDA)

This agreement defines operational conditions, responsibilities, and constraints for deployment of contracted services and associated assets.

1. Scope of Services

Covers deployment of agreed services including system setup, integration, operational support, and maintenance during the active contract period.

2. Deployment Conditions

All services are deployed only after written authorization and confirmation of technical readiness, including infrastructure availability and access permissions.

3. Client Responsibilities

Clients are responsible for providing accurate requirements, required access credentials, site readiness, and compliance with applicable regulations.

4. Operational Constraints

Service delivery may be limited by environmental conditions, infrastructure limitations, third-party dependencies, or regulatory restrictions.

5. Service Limitations

The provider is not responsible for failures caused by external systems, unauthorized modifications, or improper use outside agreed scope.

6. Data Handling

All operational data generated during service delivery is handled according to agreed retention, access, and confidentiality terms.

7. Suspension & Termination

Services may be suspended or terminated in cases of non-payment, breach of terms, safety concerns, or legal requirement.

8. Liability Terms

Liability is limited to direct damages within the contracted service scope. Indirect or consequential damages are excluded where permitted by law.
