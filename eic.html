Enterprise Integration Contract (EIC)

This contract defines requirements and responsibilities for integration of external enterprise systems with Black Steel Innovations infrastructure.

1. Integration Scope

Defines the systems, platforms, and services that will be connected under this agreement, including required interfaces and supported environments.

2. Technical Requirements

All integration must meet compatibility standards including supported APIs, authentication methods, and data exchange formats agreed prior to deployment.

3. Security Requirements

All connected systems must comply with access control policies, encryption standards, and identity verification procedures defined in the security agreement.

4. Data Exchange Terms

Data transfer between systems must follow defined protocols including rate limits, validation rules, and permitted data categories.

5. System Dependencies

Both parties acknowledge that system availability may depend on third-party services, infrastructure providers, or external network conditions.

6. Maintenance & Updates

Scheduled maintenance windows and update procedures must be coordinated to avoid disruption of integrated systems where possible.

7. Compliance Obligations

All integrations must comply with applicable data protection, cybersecurity, and operational regulations relevant to the deployment region.

8. Termination Conditions

Integration may be suspended or terminated due to breach of contract, security risk, or operational instability affecting system integrity.
